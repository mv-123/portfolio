Classic Genre

A classic is a book that is well-written and has cultural importance. Classics can be prose works, such as novels or short stories, or non-fiction genres, such as histories, biographies, or religious texts. Classics can be appreciated for their construction and literary art. They should be of high artistic quality, at least for the time in which they were written. Classics can reflect a time period, a societal standard, or may offer commentary on a subject.
